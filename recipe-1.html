butter chiken recipe

ingrediants

  1. 1 1/2 pounds boneless chiken
  2. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  3. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  4. 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  5. 2 teaspoons garam masala powder
  6. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  7. 1 teaspoon coriander powder

step by step procces to make take 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 medium tomatoes, 1 tablespoon butter, 2 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil 2 tablespoons tomato puree 1 tablespoon garam masala powder 1 teaspoon cumin powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional) Salt, to taste 1 cup chicken broth 1/2 cup heavy cream Fresh cilantro, for garnish Marinate the chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala powder, cumin powder, and salt for at least 30 minutes. Cook the chicken in a pan until it's browned and cooked through. In the same pan, add the diced tomatoes, butter, chicken broth, and heavy cream. Stir until the sauce thickens. Add the cooked chicken back into the sauce and simmer for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with basmati rice or naan bread.
